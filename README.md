## Project 1: World Population Dashboard

This Tableau HR Analytics dashboard provides an interactive overview of **workforce attrition and employee demographics**, helping stakeholders identify key patterns related to employee turnover. The dashboard highlights overall attrition rate, total and active employees, average age, and attrition distribution by gender.

Users can explore attrition trends across departments, education fields, age groups, and job roles to uncover areas with higher employee turnover. Key visuals include department-wise and education field-wise attrition breakdowns, age group distribution, and job satisfaction ratings by role.

The dashboard also enables deeper analysis of attrition by gender across different age bands, supporting data-driven HR decisions related to retention strategies, workforce planning, and employee engagement.

## Project 2: Unicorn Companies Global Overview Dashboard

This Tableau dashboard presents an interactive **analysis of global wildlife** data, focusing on animal lifespan, speed, threat levels, habitats, and geographical distribution. It provides high-level metrics such as average lifespan, average and maximum speed, total animals, and threat level counts.

Users can explore the fastest animals, compare lifespan across animal types, and analyse threat levels by continent. A world map visual highlights species distribution by country, while flow diagrams illustrate the relationship between habitats and animal types.

Key visuals include bar charts, donut charts, stacked bar charts, maps, and flow diagrams, enabling users to gain insights into biodiversity patterns, conservation risks, and wildlife characteristics across regions.
